We know that selling is not everyone's bag, and selling yourself is certainly something very few people are comfortable with. Whether you're a newly minted designer or a seasoned pro moving roles, spending time sprucing up your interview techniques is an investment worth making. Interview Coaching 101 will give you the confidence to know you're already laid the groundwork and can take on any interview that comes your way.

We'll ask for:
  • Links to web or PDF portfolios when you book and no later than 72 hrs before your session
  • Your CV
  • Your LinkedIn profile
We'll give you:
  • A clear idea of which questions you should ask at interview and why
  • A post-session question deck of commonly asked design interview questions
  • Actionable points to improve your interview skills
  • A better understanding of narrative and how to tell your story
  • How to navigate remote interviews
  • Ask practice questions and analysis of your responses
  • A recording of your session (we know you hate it üôà, but it's important to know how others see you)
Options: OPTION 1: One Design focussed coach OPTION 2: Two coaches, either both Design focussed, or one Design and one Business focussed
  • Design interviewers will be in a leadership role in UX/Product Design, Business interviewers in a leadership role within Business Consultancy
  • All coaches have experience in candidate sourcing and interviewing at senior and executive levels

ü§ì Interview Mastery

This session is specifically for people who have an interview lined up, and would like time practice and impartial advice to feel totally prepared. As such this is a completely bespoke session, tailored to the individual interview at hand.

We'll ask for:
  • Links to web or PDF portfolios when you book and no later than 72 hrs before your session
  • Your CV
  • Your LinkedIn profile
  • The name of the company that has invited you to interview
  • The job description of the role you are interviewing for
  • Any information about the interview that the company has sent you (including the design challenge if applicable)
We'll give you:
  • Opportunity to answer interview questions crafted to exemplify the type of questions you will be asked by the specific company you are interviewing with
  • Live analysis of your responses
  • Advice on how to create positive narratives from any career changes or gaps you may have
  • Opportunity to walkthrough and get feedback on your design challenge (if applicable)
